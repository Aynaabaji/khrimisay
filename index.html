Who we are

We are Khrimisay and we are dedicated to making the social media space fun and memorable.

Why we are

Our purpose is to make social media fun and harness the true power of friendships as we give an avenue to share thoughts with others in localities and beyond.

How we are

We are dedicated to our users and this dedication is the basis of all of our policies and terms. To ensure we stay true to this, we have established practical ways to ensure everyone has a good time using Khrimisay without feeling muffled or offended. When users come across the terms “Speak Smoothly Rational“ or “Usercracy” they can smile knowingly either in expectation or acknowledgement because it shows how seriously Khrimisay is taking its commitment to making sure their thoughts are heard when they share them, and ensuring others around the world can adequately interact and respond.
